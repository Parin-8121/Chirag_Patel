Process

How We Work Together

1

Discovery Call

A short conversation to understand your situation, goals, and timelines. We’ll confirm whether we’re the right fit and outline next steps.

2

Document Review

You securely share relevant financial information, past tax returns, or bookkeeping records for review.

3

Plan & Proposal

We present a clear plan and fee structure, including scope, deliverables, and timing.

4

Implementation

We complete the agreed-upon work: tax filings, financial statements, advisory reports, or support with CRA and external stakeholders.

5

Ongoing Support

We remain available for questions, future planning, and additional services as your needs evolve.
