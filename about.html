About Chirag Patel, CPA

A Trusted CPA for Woodbridge and the GTA

Chirag Patel is a Chartered Professional Accountant based in Woodbridge, Ontario, providing tax, accounting, and advisory services to individuals, families, and businesses across the Greater Toronto Area.

With more than 15 years of experience in public practice and industry, Chirag combines technical expertise with a practical, relationship-first approach. Clients appreciate clear explanations, timely communication, and strategies tailored to their specific situation.

Professional Focus

  • Owner-managed businesses and professional corporations
  • Tax planning for business owners and their families
  • Financial reporting for lenders, investors, and boards
  • Support for non-profit organizations and charities

Approach & Values

The firm is built on three core principles: clarity, compliance, and confidence. Every engagement is designed to help clients understand their numbers, meet their obligations, and make informed decisions for the future.
